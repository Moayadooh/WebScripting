Computing Department

Department of Computing is established in 1996, offering three bachelor degree programmes with honours in affiliation with University of Stirling (UoS), UK, two bachelor degree programmes with honours in affiliation with University Malaysia Pahang (UMP), Malaysia and two Higher National Diploma (HND) programmes with Scottish Qualifications Authority SQA, UK.

These programmes provide students with a comprehensive, well-planned and balanced learning experience based on the current job market in Sultanate of Oman.

The department is equipped with state-of-the-art classrooms and computer labs. There are dedicated labs for students to gain practical experience in networking, hardware maintenance, CISCO router configuration.

Computing Department is to prepare students to be:
  • Ready to be absorbed by the industry to start a career in the field of Information and Communication Technology
  • Able to start an entrepreneurship in the field of Information Technology.
  • Able to pursue research activities in the field of Information Technology.
