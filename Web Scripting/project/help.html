Help & Support

  • How to buy a car?
    • Click on "View Cars" option, than you will find many cars photo with information and owner details.
    Contact with car owner and make a deal.

  • How to display your car for sale in website?
    • Click on "View Account" option, enter your user name and password to login to the website, (if you dont have account click on sign up, create your new account and login to the website), than select add button, fill the fields "Type of car, Model, Top Spedd, Color, Price, Name, Mobile No", select your car photo, than click on upload button.

  • How to modify your car details?
    • Click on "View Account" option and login to the website, than click on "Edit" button of the car which you want to modify its data, than after modify click on "Save" button.

  • How to delete your car from the website?
    • Click on "View Account" option and login to the website, than click on "Delete" button of the car which you want to delete it, than click on "Save" button.
