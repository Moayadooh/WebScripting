Departments

1.ESL & General Foundation Department:

The English program at our ESL Dept. is open to all non-native speakers of English who wish to develop their language skills, pursue their studies in higher education, or further their professional careers.

2.Mathematics and Computer Science Department:

The Department of Mathematics and Computer Science offers Bachelor’s Degree Programs in Computer Science, Information systems, Information Security and Statistics, which provide students with high-quality education and prepare them for long and successful careers in industry and government.

3.Business & Economics Department:

The Department of Business and Economics is actively involved in providing high-quality teaching within the fields of Economics, Business, Aviation, Health and Safety at the Bachelor’s level. The main study programs in the department are: Bachelor of Science in Business Administration, Accounting, Airport Management, Aviation Management, Commercial Law, and Health and Safety.
